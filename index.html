ABOUT US

Over the last year, the healthcare workers at the University of Michigan Hospital have selflessly and valiantly devoted their lives to helping their patients in the face of the COVID-19 pandemic. As undergraduate students, we feel it is now our turn to step up and say thanks to all of incredible individuals who have helped keep our communities safe during these trying times. Our goal is to create an EPIC display filled with heartfelt messages from the student body for the Michigan healthcare workers as a way of expressing our gratitude.

We want our THANK YOU to be loud to overemphasize our gratitude to our selfless and valiant healthcare workers! Using massive letters 8 feet tall and 15 feet wide, these letters will contain hundreds (or thousands) of plastic balls each with a message written on it. When the project is over, the words THANK YOU will be colorful array of appreciation from the Michigan community to let our heros know that their community has their support in these unprecdented times.
